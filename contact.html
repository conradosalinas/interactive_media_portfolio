ABOUT CONRADO

Conrado Salinas is a Los Angeles based illustrator with a background in graphic design.

Conrado was featured in Versus Magazine's 2014 Summer Issue and had his 'Bone Collector' piece printed and featured at Adobe MAX 2014. He was also featured and created an 18 step portrait tutorial for Advanced Photoshop's October 2014 issue.

Conrado is expected to graduate in 2015 with a Bachelors of Arts degree in Graphic Design.
