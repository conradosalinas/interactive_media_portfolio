About Hausen

Hausen’s goal is to become one of the world’s leading premium lifestyle brands; to deliver superior styling, quality and value to consumers worldwide. The brand hopes to provide a refreshing twist to fashion by finding the balance between the snobby fashion elites, and the streetwear & urban scene.

We invite you to join us on this noble journey! Expect excellence, Hausen.
